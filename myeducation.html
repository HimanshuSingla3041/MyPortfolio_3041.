What I Have Earned Yet

My journey of discipline, resilience, and continuous learning

I began my academic journey under the CBSE Board, completing my 10th standard at Yaduvanshi Shiksha Niketan, Gurgaon and my 12th standard at SND Public School, Palwal. With the dream of serving the nation, I moved to Dehradun, where I joined the Doon Defence Academy (DDA) for NDA coaching and competitive exam preparation. Although I could not secure a place at NDA, the journey taught me discipline, resilience, and courage. I also took a one-year drop, during which I kept striving with patience and determination.

By the grace of God and through persistent effort, I earned admission to Birla Institute of Technology and Science (BITS), where I am currently a third-year Computer Science Engineering student.
